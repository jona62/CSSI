Description:

This is a safe haven for troubled teens and young adults, where they may freely vent about their troubles in regards to the verious mental issues stated below. All discussions are anonymous, allowing visitors to focus solely on working on their issues. If there is a topic you'd like to have a discussion on that's not listed below, visit our contacts page and drop us an email, we would be happy to add it in for you.


Topics of Discussion:

  1. Social Phobia (or Social Anxiety Disorder)
  2. Social (Pragmatic) Communication Disorder
  3. Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)
  4. Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD)
  5. Generalized Anxiety Disorder
  6. Reading Disorder
  7. Panic Disorder
  8. Stuttering
  9. Selective Mutism
  10. Schizophrenia
  11. Generalized Anxiety Disorder
  12. Stereotypic Movement Disorder
